Help

With wifi Web UI you can plug your raspberry pi's LAN interface to the WAN interface of your fitzbox and share a nearby wifi.

All you have to do is to configure the SSID and the PSK (pre shared key) of the wifi you want to bridge to your fitzbox (1:1 NAT) and your're done. After that you can connect to your fitzbox's wifi or LAN to use the internet as usual.

To configure the SSID and the PSK go to settings => wifi in the main menu.
